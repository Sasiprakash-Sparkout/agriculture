about the agriculture

Agriculture is the backbone of human survival and civilization.

It provides food, nutrition, employment, and raw materials for many industries.

Agriculture plays a vital role in supporting the economy, ensuring food security, and improving rural development.

It also contributes to international trade through the export of agricultural products such as rice, cotton, and sugarcane.

By adopting sustainable farming practices and modern technology, agriculture helps protect the environment while meeting the growing needs of the world's population.
